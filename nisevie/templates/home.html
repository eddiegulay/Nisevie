What is Nisevie ?

CRDB Nisevie is One of the Savings banking service, through which Customers would set up Savings process to Excute Automatically

Automatic Savings Process

Using Plan Advisor to Calculate amount you can save

Through Plan Advisor section you will be able to fill in your Income streams as well as your Expenses. You will also provide some further details so that the optimal amount to be saved can be calculated

Going directly to Create Plan

Form will be provided to fill in your income stream and the amount you want to be deduced from that particular income After submitting the Deduction Excution process will be done Automatically up until the End date you provided

Service Termination

Incase of Emergencies you will always be able to Terminate the service (works for flexible plans)
